William Gumede is Associate Professor, School of Governance, University of the Witwatersrand, Johannesburg. He is the Chairperson of the <a href="www.democracyworks.org.za">Democracy Works Foundation</a>. One of his recent books is Restless Nation - Making Sense of Troubled Times (Tafelberg).
